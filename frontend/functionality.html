Inside the AI

How Our Models Assess Roads, Bridges, and Buildings

Road Assessment

1. Image Acquisition

  • Captures images using drones, satellite imagery, or mobile cameras.

2. Preprocessing

  • Noise Reduction: Enhances clarity.
  • Edge Detection: Identifies cracks and potholes.
  • Segmentation: Separates road surfaces from other elements.

3. Feature Extraction

  • Detects cracks, potholes, surface wear, and lane markings.
  • Uses CNNs for automated feature detection.

4. Condition Classification

  • Classifies roads as Good, Moderate, or Poor based on condition.

Bridge Assessment

1.Input Collection:

  • The model takes textual or structured input about the bridge's condition.
  • Parameters may include visible cracks, year constructed, if flared, and previous maintenance records.
  • Weather & Traffic Data: Considers impact of climate and load on wear.

2. Preprocessing & Feature Extraction

  • Noise Reduction: Removes irrelevant data from images & sensor readings.
  • Edge Detection: Identifies cracks and material degradation.
  • Segmentation: Differentiates bridge components (deck, cables, piers, etc.).

3. Condition Classification:

  • The model processes the input and assigns a rating:
  • Safe – No major issues detected.
  • Moderate Risk – Some signs of wear and minor issues.
  • High Risk – Critical structural concerns requiring immediate attention.

4. Output

  • Displays the bridge's condition rating.

Building Assessment

1. Data Collection

  • Images & Videos: Captured from drones, CCTV, or handheld cameras.
  • Thermal Imaging: Detects heat leaks, insulation problems, and internal structural weaknesses.
  • LiDAR & 3D Scanning: Creates 3D models to check for deformations.
  • Vibration & Seismic Sensors: Detect unusual movements or stress points.
  • Sound & Ultrasonic Sensors: Identify hidden cracks or voids inside walls.

2. Preprocessing

  • Noise Reduction: Removes irrelevant details from images and sensor data.
  • Segmentation: Separates walls, pillars, beams, and floors for targeted analysis.
  • Edge Detection: Highlights cracks, misalignments, and material fatigue.
  • Anomaly Detection: Compares current structure to ideal or past states.

3. Feature Extraction

  • Cracks & Surface Damage: Measures depth, width, and spread of cracks.
  • Material Degradation: Detects corrosion, rust, or weakened concrete.
  • Structural Shifts: Identifies tilting, bending, or sinking foundations.
  • Load-Bearing Analysis: Checks stress distribution on beams and columns.
  • Thermal & Moisture Leaks: Identifies heat escape zones or water seepage.

4. Condition Classification

  • Safe: No major issues, normal wear and tear.
  • Moderate Risk: Some cracks or minor damage, requiring maintenance.
  • High Risk: Severe structural issues, requiring urgent repair or evacuation.

5. Predicting Lifespan & Risk Assessment

  • Uses machine learning regression models to estimate the remaining lifespan of the building.
  • Predicts failure risks under different conditions (e.g., earthquakes, heavy loads, weathering).
  • Provides risk heatmaps highlighting the most vulnerable areas.
